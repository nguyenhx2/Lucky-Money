## Getting Started

### 1. Access

Open your browser and go to the URL provided by your admin.  
Supported: Chrome, Safari, Firefox — desktop and mobile.

### 2. Sign In

Click **"Sign in with Google"** and select your Google account.

> **Note:** Only pre-approved emails can participate. If you see an "Access Denied" screen, contact your admin to be added to the list.

---

## Picking an Envelope

### Main Screen

After signing in, you'll see:

- 🎋 **Envelope grid** hanging on bamboo poles — envelopes sway gently
- 🎵 **Tết music** auto-plays (or press ▶ at the bottom-left)
- 🧧 **Pick button** — tap any envelope to select it

### How to Pick

1. **Tap any envelope** on the screen
2. Wait for the opening animation (1–2 seconds)
3. **Result appears** — your lucky amount + Tết greeting + card
4. Tap **"Close"** or swipe down to return

### Countdown

If the session hasn't started yet, you'll see a countdown timer. Wait for the time set by your admin!

## Special Features

### 🎰 Secret Bonus Round

Some lucky players selected by admin receive a **second secret draw** after the regular pick:

1. After viewing your result, a purple **"Draw More"** button appears
2. Tap it to receive your special bonus reward
3. Result displays as a **3D flip card** in purple theme

### 🔄 Extra Turn (Retry)

Admin can grant extra turns to specific players. If you receive one:
- After finishing your pick, a **"Pick Again"** button appears
- Tap it to return to the envelope grid for another selection

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Retry grants are single-use. Once you pick an envelope with a retry grant, it's consumed.

### 💰 Donate Fund Badge

If the session uses a community fund, you'll see:
- A **gold circular button** at the screen corner — tap to view the fund
- Shows total fund, list of contributors
- If you're a contributor: view prize distribution stats and winners list

## Viewing Past Results

If you've used all your turns, the screen switches to **"AlreadyPicked"** — showing:
- Total amount received
- Flip card to review regular and bonus envelopes
- Tết greeting and card

## FAQ

**Q: I tapped an envelope but someone else claimed it?**  
A: The system prevents 2 people from claiming the same envelope simultaneously (race-safe). That envelope hides automatically — pick another one.

**Q: I don't see any envelopes?**  
A: Either all envelopes have been claimed, or the session hasn't started yet (check the countdown).

**Q: Where's my congratulatory email?**  
A: Email is sent automatically after picking. Check your spam folder if it's not in your inbox.

**Q: I see "Access Denied"?**  
A: Your email is not on the approved list. Contact admin to be added.
